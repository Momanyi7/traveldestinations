Stunning Destinations

There are many stunning destinations around the world that are worth visiting. Here are some of them based on various sources.

Paris, France

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower.
  • Explore the Louvre Museum.
  • Take a Seine River cruise.

Santorini, Greece.

  • Relax on the beaches.
  • Explore Oia's sunset views.
  • Visit ancient Thera.

Machu Picchu, Peru.

  • Hike the Inca Trail.
  • Explore the ruins of Machu Picchu.
  • Visit the Sun Gate.

Kyoto, Japan.

  • Visit Kinkaku-ji Temple.
  • Explore Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
  • Experience a traditional tea ceremony.

New York City, USA.

  • Walk through Central Park.
  • Visit the Statue of Liberty.
  • Explore Times Square.

Sydney, Australia.

  • Visit the Sydney Opera House.
  • Relax at Bondi Beach.
  • Explore the Royal Botanic Garden.

Serengeti, Tanzania.

  • Go on a safari to see the Big Five.
  • Witness the Great Migration.
  • Hot air balloon ride over the plains.

Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe.

  • Take a helicopter ride over the falls.
  • White-water rafting in the Zambezi River.
  • Bungee jumping off the Victoria Falls Bridge.

Pyramids of Giza, Egypt.

  • Explore the ancient pyramids.
  • Visit the Sphinx.
  • Take a camel ride around the area.

Masai Mara National Reserve, Kenya.

  • Experience a traditional Maasai village.
  • Hot air balloon safari over the reserve.
  • Witness the annual wildebeest migration.

Table Mountain, South Africa.

  • Hike to the top for stunning views.
  • Abseil down the mountain.
  • Take a cable car ride to the summit.

Okavango Delta, Botswana.

  • Mokoro (dugout canoe) safari through the delta.
  • Game viewing by boat.
  • Walking safaris on the delta's islands.
